About Pass Keeper

Welcome to Pass Keeper, your simple and secure solution for managing passwords efficiently. Our application allows you to store, retrieve, and manage your credentials safely using local storage in your browser.

Pass Keeper is designed for users who want an easy-to-use, client-side password manager without the hassle of cloud synchronization. Your passwords are stored locally, ensuring privacy and security.

With features like password masking, easy deletion, and a clean interface, Pass Keeper is the perfect tool to help you organize your credentials without compromising security.
